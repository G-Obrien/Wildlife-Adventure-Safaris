ABOUT US

Africa Tours adventure Ltd is a local tour operator based in Uganda.
We focus on Gorilla trekking Holidays in both Rwanda and Uganda, extending tours to Burundi, Eastern DR Congo, Tanzania and Kenya. We also organise nature tours, cultural tours, birding safaris, wildlife viewing safaris, adventure travel among other activities.
Our aim is to give you the natural feeling of a holiday in Africa
